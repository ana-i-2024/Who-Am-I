About

Welcome! I’ve always been fascinated by what makes people tick (hello, psychology degree from the olden days!), but now I’m learning how to make websites tick too. I’m not exactly a coding expert yet — think "digital toddler" more than "tech wizard" — but I’m getting there! With my newfound love for front-end development, I’ve created this fun, interactive quiz to help you explore your own personality quirks. And as a proud fur parent to a Cavoodle, I couldn’t resist adding a dog API that shows random images of dogs — because, really, who doesn’t love a surprise dose of dog cuteness? So, while I’m busy getting all these pixels in line, why not take the quiz and find out who you really are?
